# Nalini Store Annual Sales Dashboard 2026

## Project Objective
The objective of this project is to analyze Nalini Store’s annual sales data for 2026 and understand customer behavior, sales trends, and business performance using Excel/Google Sheets.

---

## Problems Solved
- Compared sales and orders using charts
- Identified highest sales and order month
- Analyzed men vs women purchases
- Studied different order statuses
- Analyzed age and gender relationship
- Identified top-performing sales channel

---

## Tools Used
- Excel / Google Sheets
- Pivot Tables
- Pivot Charts
- Data Visualization

---

## Analysis
- March recorded the highest sales and orders.
- Women customers contributed 64% of total sales.
- Adult customers placed the maximum orders.
- Amazon was the top-performing sales channel.
- 92.3% of orders were successfully delivered.

---

## Conclusion
The dashboard helps Nalini Store understand customer purchasing behavior, identify top-performing channels, and improve future business strategies for sales growth.
